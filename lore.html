Lore

Xtreme Chaser is a fighting tournament held once every century in Elysium. A no-man's land where God first lands on earth.
Xtreme Chaser was said to be pioneered by the so-called “Ancient Family” who's been told as God's descendant for those who seeks the Mark of Domination.
Legend said, ones who can prove themselves will be granted an ultimate power.
The New World is looking for a new champion in reign, thus many fighters around the world gathered on Elysium joining Xtreme Chaser to prove themselves and become the strongest in the world.
Countless battles day and night and only a select few will survived.
Who is going to be the strongest in the world?
That’s what Xtreme Chaser is after.
